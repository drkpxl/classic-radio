# Denver / Golden, CO — Radio Station Reference

Working list for picking the **preset stations** the tuner will cycle through.
Scope: **FM broadcast + HD Radio + NOAA Weather Radio** only.

> Genres kept per your call: **cut country, Christian, Spanish**. You'll dial in
> the final presets manually from the rest. Aim for ~6–12 presets.

Legend: ⚡ = station is known/likely to broadcast **HD Radio** (extra HD2/HD3 subchannels possible).
Reception note: a few entries are **Colorado Springs market** (~60 mi south) and may receive poorly in Golden — flagged inline.

| ☐ | 162.475 | WXM51  | Mead/Longmont | North metro |
| ☐ | 162.400 | WXM50  | Greeley   | Far north / Weld Co. |
